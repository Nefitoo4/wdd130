1. Does the website work as a travel agency?

No, this website does not work as travel agency, travelworld is just a community website that join the opinion of all travelers around the world to share their opinions and experiences to help other travelers and adventurers as a trip advisor.

2. Does this website charge any commission for trip consultancy?

No, the use of this website is free and we don't charge any commission of any kind either voluntary payment. The only thing we ask our travelers is to share their experiences of their trip through our social media channel or email to update this website every day and week to improve the website and bring you important information for your trip.

3. Does travelworld is immigration agency?

No, travelworld is not an immigration agency, we don't provide consultancy to travelers which purpose is to immigrate to other country to look any refugee status, citizenship, work visas, residency, this is not the right website, to know the required documentation for the entrance of the country you woul like to visit we recommend you to visit the embassy website or the closes consulate in your country.

4. If I don't find the information I wnated to know about the country or place, where can I go to?

We sorry if for any reason you don't find the information you wanted to know of the country or places you wanted to know however we encourage you to look our channels in social media or email to send us a question or comment and we will respond as soon as possible we can with as much information you need.

5. Does the website issue documentation to travel the country I want?

Travelworld will never issue documentation to help you travel to the country you want to do your trip. We recommend you to visit you migration consulate or secretary of foreigners and migration for them issue the documentation you need to realize your trip.
